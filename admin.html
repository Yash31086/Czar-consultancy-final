How This Works

1. Edit any section using the sidebar on the left.
2. Click "Save Changes" in each section.
3. Go to "Publish Changes" and copy the generated code.
4. Paste it into your index.html at the marked location and re-upload to Vercel.
Note: Changes are saved here in your browser until you publish them into the site file.
